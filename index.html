GATES isn’t just an event for us, it is in fact a journey, a festival that enables us to put our best foot forward and be as culturally active especially on the dates of the event. It helps define ourselves, find our paths and most importantly take something from it to cherish as a team! Our motto is to let this event be a platform which would help the students, faculty, participants and the sponsors acquaint and interact with each other, forming bonds stronger than those made on paper. Last time we gained all of that by finding a team we could trust and most importantly who had each other’s back! A team must always have a dose of synergy that strives for excellence. This year we’re back with double energy and enthusiasm to make this event an absolute success for everyone. So, we invite all the sponsors to have trust in us and get associated with us.

Flashback

Gates 2009

Breaking our own records each year, GATES 2009 witnessed one of the most overwhelming participation. With more than 30 events every day from all spheres be it technical, literary, cultural or sports.

Gates 2010

With improved infrastructure GATES 2010 stood out as the most attended GATES of all time and participation reaching more than 5000.

Star Night
- Diljit Dosanjh

Gates 2011

It set its own benchmark regarding participation and events. It saw participation from colleges from various universities across northern India.

Gates 2012

GATES achieved new landmarks every year since its inception and this year was no different, bringing participants from all spheres to showcase their talent.

Gates 2013

With ever growing popularity it became avenue to initiate, build and maintain acquaintances in the academic and industrial arena.

Gates 2014

Growing steadily to become what it is today, the fest has come a long way becoming a totem of the technical and cultural prowess of GTBITians.

Star Night
- Sharry Mann

Gates 2016

In the history of GTBIT, it was the first time GATES witnessed Gurdaas Maan, Mankirat Aulakh and Dilpreet Dhillon live, all at once. The never ending enthusiasm and successful events is what made GATES 2016 a grand success.


Star Night
- Dilpreet Dhillon
- Mankirt Aulakh

Gates 2019

The latest GATES won all hearts with a fun filled competition, Mr&Ms Gates experiencing a huge participation and of course, singers Ninja and Guri enthralled our audiences with their power packed performances of their hit numbers.
